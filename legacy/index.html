Core Capabilities

Everything MediVault Offers

Medical-grade software built around people — patients, doctors, and hospitals alike.

Patient Controlled Records

Patients fully own and manage their medical history. Grant or revoke access to anyone, anytime.

Secure Medical Storage

Encrypted digital vault for all health reports. AES-256 encryption ensures your data is always locked tight.

Emergency Access Protocol

Doctors can access critical records instantly during emergencies — blood group, allergies, medications. 24-hour window, fully audited.

Hospital Integration

Hospitals can upload reports directly into patient vaults. Seamless transfers between institutions with full consent workflows.

Transparent Audit Logs

Every record access is logged and visible to the patient. Immutable, tamper-proof — not even admins can delete entries.

Universal Medical Identity

A single patient profile accessible across hospitals and clinics. One identity, all your records, everywhere you go.
